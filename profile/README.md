# OpenEnvision

OpenEnvision is an open research initiative focused on vision intelligence, multimodal AI, world models, and physical intelligence.

We bring together researchers, students, engineers, and institutional partners to advance AI research through papers, code, models, datasets, benchmarks, and public project pages.

## Mission

OpenEnvision advances vision-centered artificial intelligence by transforming research insight into open models, systems, data, and evaluation frameworks for the broader AI community.
